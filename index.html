Course Overview 🌟

Welcome to "Data Visualization with Tableau"! This course will guide you through the art and science of transforming raw data into insightful, interactive visualizations using Tableau. Perfect for beginners and intermediate learners, we'll cover installation, basic graphs, and advanced techniques to bring your data to life! 🚀

To begin, you’ll need Tableau Public installed on your device. Download it here and follow the installation instructions. This software is essential for all course activities! ⚙️
